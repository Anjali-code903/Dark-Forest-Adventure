The dark forest

You wake up shivering under an old oak tree. The sun is setting, and you hear a strange howl in the distance. There are two paths ahead of you: a muddy trail heading into the deep woods, and a rocky path leading up a mountain.

What do you do?

  • Follow the muddy trail into the woods.
  • Climb the rocky path up the mountain.












The deep woods

The canopy of trees blocks out the last bit of daylight. Suddenly, a pair of glowing yellow eyes stares back at you from the bushes!

What do you do ?

  • Approach the glowing eyes slowly.
  • Panic and run back to the start!














The mountain path

The wind howls as you climb higher. The path ends abruptly at the entrance of a dark, eerie cave, but you can see the stars clearly from up here.

What do you do?

  • Step inside the dark, eerie cave.
  • Turn around and run back to the start!
















Ending: The Forest Guardian

As you step forward, a massive, silver wolf steps out of the bushes. It doesn't attack. Instead, it drops a glowing golden key at your feet, bows its head, and walks away into the fog. You survived, and you found a mystery!
